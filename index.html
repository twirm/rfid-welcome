SXSW RFID Crew 2017

What do we do?

  • Deploy Android tablets to venues around Austin for SXSW
  • Provide venues with the ability to check badges of attendees
  • Help venues with any problems they have with the devices

What is RFID?

RFID stands for Radio Frequency IDentification

It is a way to communicate data from a SXSW badge to the device checking for the badges authenticity
